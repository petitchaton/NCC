New Climate Culture

Thru art, culture, and climate science, New Climate Culture has developed entirely new standards. These new standards of industry are capable of synthesizing the old world with the new, for future technological development and conscious lifestyle curation.



With New Climate Culture, our international team of in-network researchers is unparalleled. We are working with the world’s leading experts in carbon sequestration, environmental remediation, sustainable agriculture, re-wilding, genetics, architecture, urban design, energy management, land stewardship, organic materials, coral farming, marine permaculture, mycology, and whole systems design.



Climate science is a rapidly developing field that we have approached from multiple disciplines rooted in permaculture technology.

Examining the entire planetary system as a living organism and responding to that in real time, that’s our specialty. Transmuting infinitely large data sets into easily processable information that leads to discreet solutions… we are truly redefining what is possible.


We help industry leaders reimagine the built environment to identify design opportunities for future economies, replacing obsolete technologies.

Elsa Worldwide is a 29 year old Climate Scientist and Futurist born and raised in the wild and spectacular deserts of Arizona. Founder: New Climate Culture.


Eloísa is the Mexican-American child of an “illegal” immigrant who was re-cultured on indigenous territories in North America within tribes such as the Apache, Navajo, and Lakota Nations.






As an artist and field researcher, Elsa lived in urban centers, off the grid, farms, homesteads, artist residencies, eco-villages, communes, schoolies, tiny houses, squats, tents, co-working spaces, and anywhere else that she found would be fertile grounds for sharing her earth-based spirituality as an ally to the global soil, water, and atmospheric systems.
